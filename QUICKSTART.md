# MOUNTAIN RISE - Quick Start Guide

## Installation & Running

### Option 1: Direct Python
```bash
python game.py
```

### Option 2: Using Launcher (Linux/Mac)
```bash
chmod +x run.sh
./run.sh
```

---

## Game Flow

### Step 1: Character Creation
- Choose your primary mount (MOOSE, HORSE, or CARIBOU)
- Each mount has unique stat bonuses:
  - **MOOSE**: Heavy hitter with most HP (120) but slower
  - **HORSE**: Fastest and well-rounded (speed 9)
  - **CARIBOU**: Best defense and stamina (DEF 10)
- Enter character name
- Two NPCs join you: Aleksandr (Horse) and Temüjin (Caribou)

### Step 2: Main Gameplay Loop
Each turn, choose an action:
1. **Travel** - Go to a new region, encounter enemies
2. **Rest** - Heal all characters by 50 HP
3. **View Stats** - Check detailed character information
4. **Save Game** - Record your progress
5. **Quit** - Exit game

### Step 3: Combat
When you encounter an enemy:
- **Attack**: Deal random damage (70-130% of attack power)
  - Critical hit chance: 15% (1.5x damage)
- **Defend**: Boost defense temporarily, reduce damage taken
- **Heal**: Recover 30 HP using medicinal herbs

### Step 4: Progression
- Win battles = Gain experience + Conquer 1 territory
- Level up when experience reaches level × 100
- Each level up grants:
  - +10 Max HP
  - +5 Attack Power  
  - +2 Defense

## Regions & Enemy Types

### Siberian Pass
- Mountain Bandit (40 HP, weak)
- Cossack Raider (50 HP, moderate)
- Pirate Scout (35 HP, fast)

### Mongolian Plateau  
- Mongol Warrior (55 HP, strong)
- Nomadic Archer (45 HP, ranged attacker)
- Steppe Warlord (70 HP, very strong)

### Ural Mountains
- Mountain Shaman (50 HP, magical)
- Yak Herder (60 HP, tanky)
- Avalanche Summoner (65 HP, dangerous)

### Lake Baikal
- Ice Wizard (55 HP, fast attacker)
- Fish Merchant (40 HP, weak)
- Frost Giant (80 HP, boss-level)

### Altai Mountains
- Golden Eagle Knight (65 HP, strong)
- Wolf Pack Leader (55 HP, fast)
- Black Dragon Guardian (100 HP, final boss)

## Strategy Tips

### Combat Strategy
1. **Early Battles**: Use Attack to finish quickly
2. **Low HP**: Use Heal action to recover
3. **Tough Enemies**: Use Defend to reduce damage
4. **Boss Battles**: Mix Defend and Heal strategically

### Progression Strategy
1. Start in Siberian Pass for easy wins
2. Build levels and gather experience
3. Progress to harder regions (Mongolian Plateau, Ural Mountains)
4. End-game challenges in Altai Mountains

### Party Management
- Only your main character fights (but you can rest the whole party)
- Keep HP above 50% for safety margin
- Heal after 2-3 tough battles
- Rest when facing major region changes
